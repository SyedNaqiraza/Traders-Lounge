Our Methodology

We incorporate Historical Data along with Sentiments taken from the Tweets of general public and feed that data into a Predictive Model


  • Sentiment Analysis

We extract tweets from twitter on regular basis for Sentiment Analysis, tweets are being fetched from Twitter through web scrapping, Preprocess that data through NLTK Python library and through TextBlob Library each tweet is is gone through the process of sentiment analysis, after that each tweet is scored between 0-1

  • Numerical Data Analysis

This is the second part of our project in which we analyze the past trend of company, we extract different indicators which are used by financial analysts from the stocks opening and closing price, mutate these indicators with sentiment score of Tweets and feed that data into state of the art Machine Learning models


We are happy to anounce that our predictive models has 97% accuracy on Apple Stocks Data
